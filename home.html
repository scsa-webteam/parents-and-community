Parents and community

A child’s school years are exciting. They can also be challenging. It helps to know what a child should be learning at school and how their learning is going to be assessed.

This area is for parents and carers. It explains how we use curriculum and standards in Western Australia. There are links to detailed information on our main websites.

What will my child learn?

The Western Australian curriculum is based on the Australian curriculum. It has been designed to meet the specific needs of students in Western Australia.

Read more about what a child will learn at school with the Western Australian curriculum.

How is my child is going?

Assessment shows what a student has learnt. From their performance we see their ability and the gaps in what they have learnt. This is how we describe their achievement.

Read more about how your child will be assessed at school.

How will my child be assessed?

Standards are linked to the curriculum and used to measure student progress and achievement. Reporting is based on these standards.

Read more about how your child’s progress will be reported.
